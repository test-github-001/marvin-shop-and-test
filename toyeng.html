Let's tell you a little
about yourself :)

MARWIN is a brand of the "Music Lover" holding, a network of family stores of a unique Family Shop format for Kazakhstan. Family stores offer the highest quality, safe and reliable products for children of all ages. The brand's mission is to strengthen the family, promote the intellectual and physical development of children — creativity, reading, educational games and communication. MARWIN clubs for children are open in stores, where interesting, creative classes with children take place every weekend. The MARWIN children's retail chain is represented by an online store and more than 20 retail stores with a total area of more than 12,000 square meters in Astana, Almaty, Aktobe, Atyrau, Aktau, Shymkent, Zyryanovsk, Kostanay, Semey, Kyzylorda, Ust-Kamenogorsk, Uralsk and Pavlodar.
